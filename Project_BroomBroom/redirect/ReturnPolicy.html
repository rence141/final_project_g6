Return Policy

At BroomBroom, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a return and refund policy to ensure that your experience with us is as smooth as possible.

1. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be in its original condition, unused, and undamaged.
  • The item must be returned within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • The item must be returned with all original packaging, tags, and accessories.

2. Non-returnable Items

Certain items are not eligible for return, including:

  • Items that have been used or damaged by the customer.
  • Gift cards or any other non-returnable items specified on the product page.

3. How to Return an Item

If you wish to return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our Customer Service team at info@broombroom.com to initiate the return process.
  2. Provide your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return.
  3. Once your return request is approved, you will receive instructions on how to ship the item back to us.
  4. Ensure that the returned item is securely packaged to avoid damage during transit.

4. Refund Process

Upon receipt of the returned item, we will inspect it to ensure that it meets the return conditions. If the return is approved:

  • The refund will be processed to your original payment method.

Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a mistake on our part (e.g., damaged or incorrect items).

5. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you would like a different product, please initiate a return for the original item and place a new order for the desired product.

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us immediately at info@broombroom.com with the following details:

  • Your order number.
  • A description of the damage or incorrect item.
  • Photos of the damaged or incorrect item.
We will provide you with a return label and cover the cost of return shipping.

7. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs for returned items are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a mistake on our part. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance for your return shipment, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
