Shipping Policy

We want your experience at BroomBroom to be as smooth as possible. Below is our shipping policy for your reference.

1. Shipping Locations

We are pleased to offer shipping to various locations worldwide. BroomBroom delivers products to most countries, including the United States, Canada, Australia, and the Philippines, among others. For international orders, please check if we deliver to your country at checkout.

2. Shipping Fees

Shipping fees depend on the destination and the size/weight of the items ordered. The total shipping cost will be calculated at checkout before you confirm your order. For specific countries, additional customs duties or import taxes may apply, which are the responsibility of the buyer.

3. Order Processing Time

After you place your order, we will process it within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and public holidays). Please note that during peak periods or holiday seasons, processing may take a little longer. We will notify you of any delays through email.

4. Shipping Methods

We use reliable and trusted shipping carriers for all deliveries, including but not limited to FedEx, DHL, UPS, and local courier services. The shipping method and carrier will be chosen based on your location and the items in your order.

5. Delivery Time

Delivery times vary based on the shipping destination, the shipping method selected, and the availability of your products. Typically, domestic orders take 3-7 business days to arrive, while international orders may take anywhere from 7-21 business days. Tracking information will be provided once your order has been shipped.

6. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email containing a tracking number and a link to track your shipment. You can also log in to your account to view tracking details for all of your orders.

7. Shipping Restrictions

Some items may be subject to shipping restrictions, depending on your location. These may include limited-edition models or items requiring special handling. If your order includes an item with shipping restrictions, we will contact you directly to discuss alternative shipping options.

8. Address Accuracy

Please ensure that the shipping address provided is correct. BroomBroom is not responsible for any delays or additional costs incurred due to incorrect or incomplete shipping information provided by the customer.

9. Lost or Damaged Items

If your order arrives damaged or you believe it has been lost in transit, please contact our customer service team immediately at info@broombroom.com. We will work with the carrier to investigate and resolve any issues. Claims for damaged or lost items must be reported within 7 days of receiving your order.
